We are a Mysore (Karnataka, India) based Firm, captivated & involved in the export of agricultural products. Under the leadership & management of Mr. Safwan Atha & Mr. Socrates R, we have established ourselves as one among the leading merchant exporters from India.

SST EXIM offers the most comprehensive, client-centric & affordable exporters of agro-based products. Total customer satisfaction has always been the main focus of our company. During our service, we always ensure that the service rendered is apt which henceforth bring value to our patrons.

Our production unit works on the basis of technology and manpower. To ensure smooth working of the manufacturing unit, we divided the process into various departments. We at SST Exim export premium quality products that match international standards. Our quality analysts conduct stringent quality control measures to ensure durability of the products. Quality of the product is checked on various parameters at every stage of production. Our main product range comprises of rice, Indian vegetables, Indian spices, Coconut products, nuts and pulses.

We procure these from certified vendors to ensure wholesomeness of the product. We have kept our network with only the best raw material producers to warrant our clients not only the best price but also the best quality products we export.

The offered product range is available in standard as well as customized specifications. Our ability to deliver large volumes within a stipulated time schedule has aided us in developing strong business alliances and relationships with a number of reputed vendors across the domestic and international market.

We have our wide distribution network spread across Middle-Eastern countries, Europe and Africa. We have an ever-growing and versatile clientele spread across the Agro & Food Commodities Industry.
